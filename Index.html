About Us

At Kick Start, we believe in the power of shoes to change lives. Our mission is to transform discarded footwear into opportunities for environmental sustainability and community support. Through innovative recycling programs and partnerships, we aim to reduce waste and inspire a more sustainable future.

Our Vision

To create a world where no shoe goes to waste, transforming discarded footwear into opportunities for environmental sustainability and community support. We envision a circular economy where every step taken contributes to a greener planet.

Our Mission

We rescue, restore, and redistribute shoes through innovative recycling programs and community partnerships. Our mission is to reduce waste, support those in need, and inspire sustainable fashion choices that make a lasting impact on our world.
