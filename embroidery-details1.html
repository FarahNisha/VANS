CAMPAIGN

Promoting Environmental Awareness and Responsibility

The campaign aims to reduce waste by collecting pre-loved shoes that would otherwise end up in landfills. It encourages the community to practice eco-friendly habits by donating usable items instead of discarding them.

Transforming Old Items into New Stories

The campaign gives new life to old shoes, showing that even discarded items can carry new hope, meaning, and beauty when paired with care and creativity. Through this, children learn valuable lessons about resourcefulness, sustainability, and valuing what they have.
